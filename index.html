How To Use UnrugGuard
Read this before you need it. This app could save your life.
1
📲
Install on Your Phone
Open UnrugGuard in Chrome. Tap the Install banner at the top. If it does not appear — tap the ⋮ menu at top right → Add to Home Screen → Install. UnrugGuard appears on your home screen like a real app and works offline.💡 Always open from your home screen — not from Chrome. This unlocks full features including phone contact import.
2
👤
Set Up Your Profile
Go to Contacts tab. Enter your name or username — max 12 characters. This appears in every SOS message. Write a custom SOS message if needed — include blood type or medical condition.💡 Keep your name short and clear. Your contacts need to know immediately who is sending the alert.
3
👥
Add Emergency Contacts (Up to 5)
Add up to 5 trusted people. Enter manually with country code e.g. +234XXXXXXXXXX. Toggle WhatsApp on for contacts who use it.

Saved contacts show their name and number clearly with an ✏️ edit button. Tap it anytime to change.⚠️ Add people who will ACT fast — someone awake at night, someone who can call for help immediately.
4
🔑
Set Your Safe Word
Choose a secret word — e.g. Pineapple. If you are in danger but cannot press SOS, call a contact and say your safe word naturally. They know you are in danger without the attacker realising.💡 Tell ALL your emergency contacts your safe word BEFORE an emergency happens.
5
⚙️
Choose Your Trigger Method
📳 Shake to Send — Shake phone 3 times in pocket. Completely invisible. Best for armed robbery or kidnapping.

🔕 Silent SOS — Press volume button 3 times. No screen change. Best for being followed.

📞 Fake Call — SOS fires silently then shows a fake incoming call. Best for lower threat situations.⚠️ The app must be OPEN for triggers to work. Open UnrugGuard before entering any risky area.
6
📳
Shake Sensitivity
In Settings choose how hard you need to shake.

🪨 Low — Very hard shake. Best for okada riders or rough roads — prevents accidental triggers from bumps.

👋 Medium — Normal firm shake. Best for most people. Default setting.

🪶 High — Light gentle shake. Best for elderly or limited hand strength. Risk: walking may trigger accidentally.

No matter the sensitivity — you must shake 3 times within 1.5 seconds. One shake never triggers SOS.💡 If you travel by okada daily — use Low sensitivity.
7
🧪
Test Mode
In Settings turn on Test Mode before practising. All alerts say [TEST] so contacts know it is a drill. Test Mode turns off automatically after 10 minutes.⚠️ Always make sure Test Mode is OFF before going into a real risky situation.
8
🔴
How SOS Works
When SOS fires:

1. Gets your GPS location instantly
2. Sends via WhatsApp to contacts with WhatsApp toggled on
3. Sends via SMS to all other contacts
4. Sends instant in-app alarm to all GuardNetwork trusted contacts automatically

If you pressed the button by mistake — a 3 second countdown appears with a Cancel button.

If SOS already fired — tap False Alarm to send: "I am safe, previous alert was a mistake."💡 If you have no phone contacts but have GuardNetwork connections — SOS still fires automatically through GuardNetwork.
9
🛡️
GuardNetwork — App to App (Max 10)
Go to Network tab and create your ID — e.g. UG-KALU-7742. Share it with trusted people. They enter your ID and send an invite. You must approve before they can see anything.

You can add up to 10 trusted connections.

Once approved:
• See each other's location as colored dots on the map
• Tap any dot to open Google Maps at their exact location
• Their phone gets an automatic alarm when you press SOS
• View Last Location even if their phone is off
• Remove anyone anytime
• If you Decline an invite by mistake — they can simply send it again💡 Both people must have UnrugGuard installed and approve each other. Nobody sees your location without your permission.
10
🗺️
The GuardNetwork Map
Inside the Network tab you will see a map with colored dots — one for each connected person whose location is available.

Tap any dot → opens Google Maps at their exact location with full streets and navigation.

Tap Last Location button on a contact card → also opens Google Maps.

Your own location appears as a pink/purple dot labeled "You".💡 For the map to show your contact's location they must open UnrugGuard with GPS on at least once after connecting.
11
📍
Last Known Location
Every time someone opens UnrugGuard their GPS location is saved automatically. If someone goes missing — tap Last Location on their card in GuardNetwork. See exactly where they were and when. Works even if their phone is now off or taken.⚠️ Both people must be connected on GuardNetwork with approved invites for this to work.
12
🌍
Emergency Numbers — Your Country
UnrugGuard automatically detects your country and shows the correct local emergency numbers on the Home screen. Tap any number to call instantly with zero data. Works worldwide.💡 112 works as the international emergency number on any phone, any network, even without a SIM card in most countries.
13
🔋
Before Entering a Risky Area
✅ Open UnrugGuard
✅ Check contacts are saved
✅ Check trigger method is correct
✅ Test Mode is OFF
✅ Charge phone above 20%
✅ Tell someone where you are going
✅ GPS is on
✅ Know your safe word⚠️ UnrugGuard cannot work if your phone is off or dead. Always charge before travelling.
