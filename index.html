How To Use UnrugGuard
Read this before you need it. Knowing how to use this app could save your life.
1
📲
Install on Your Phone
Open UnrugGuard in Chrome on your Android phone. Tap the Install banner at the top or tap the browser menu (3 dots) → Add to Home Screen. UnrugGuard will appear on your home screen like a real app and works offline.💡 Installing gives you the best experience — faster, full screen, and works without internet after first load.
2
👤
Set Up Your Profile
Go to Contacts tab and enter your name or username. This name appears in every SOS message your contacts receive. You can also write a custom SOS message — include your blood type or medical condition if it is important.💡 Keep your name short and clear. In an emergency your contacts need to know immediately who is sending the alert.
3
👥
Add Emergency Contacts
Add up to 5 trusted people. Tap Open Phone Contacts to import directly — no typing needed. Or enter name and phone number manually with country code e.g. +234XXXXXXXXXX. Toggle WhatsApp on for contacts who use it. Once saved each contact shows their name clearly with an edit ✏️ button to change anytime.⚠️ Add people who will ACT fast — not just people you know. Someone who is awake at night. Someone who can call for help immediately.
4
🔑
Set Your Safe Word
Choose a secret word — e.g. Pineapple. If you are in danger but cannot press SOS, call a trusted contact and say your safe word naturally in the conversation. They will know you are in danger without the attacker realising.💡 Tell ALL your emergency contacts your safe word before an emergency happens. Agree on it together.
5
⚙️
Choose Your Trigger Method
Go to Settings and pick your trigger. Change it based on your situation:

📳 Shake to Send — Shake phone 3 times in your pocket. Nothing shows on screen. Best for armed robbery or kidnapping.

🔕 Silent SOS — Press volume button 3 times. No screen change. Best for being followed or watched.

📞 Fake Call — SOS fires silently then shows a fake incoming call. Best for lower threat situations.⚠️ The app must be OPEN for triggers to work. Open UnrugGuard before entering any risky area.
6
🔴
How SOS Works
When SOS fires: 1) Gets your GPS location instantly. 2) Builds a Google Maps link. 3) Sends SMS to all phone number contacts at once. 4) Sends instant in-app alert to all GuardNetwork trusted contacts. 5) Their phones receive a loud alarm automatically — no extra taps needed.

SMS works with zero data on any network worldwide. If GPS fails the message still sends without a location link.💡 Your last known location is always saved every time you open the app — even if GPS fails during emergency.
7
🛡️
GuardNetwork — App to App
Go to the Network tab and create your GuardNetwork ID — e.g. UG-KALU-7742. Share this ID with trusted people. They enter your ID in their app and send you an invite. You must approve the invite before they can see anything.

Once connected:
• You can see each other's live location on a map
• When you press SOS their phone rings automatically
• They see your exact location immediately
• You can see their last known location even if their phone is off💡 GuardNetwork only works between people who BOTH have UnrugGuard installed and have approved each other.
8
📍
Last Known Location
Every time someone opens UnrugGuard their location is saved automatically. If someone goes missing — open their profile in GuardNetwork and tap Last Location. You will see exactly where they were and when. This works even if their phone is now off, taken, or destroyed.⚠️ This requires both people to be connected on GuardNetwork with approved invites.
9
🌍
Emergency Numbers — Your Country
UnrugGuard automatically detects your country and shows the correct local emergency numbers on the Home screen. Tap any number to call instantly — works with zero data. Supported countries include Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, UK, USA, India, Germany, France and more.
10
🔋
Before Entering a Risky Area
✅ Open UnrugGuard
✅ Check contacts are saved
✅ Check trigger method is correct
✅ Charge phone above 20%
✅ Tell one contact where you are going
✅ Know your safe word
✅ Keep GPS on while travelling⚠️ UnrugGuard cannot work if your phone is off or battery is dead. Always charge before travelling.
