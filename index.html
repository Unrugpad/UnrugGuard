How To Use UnrugGuard
Read this before you need it. Knowing how to use this app can save your life.
1
👥
Set Up Your Contacts First
Go to the Contacts tab and add up to 5 people you trust. Import directly from your phone contacts — no typing needed. Add your name and an optional custom SOS message. Include your blood type or medical condition if important.💡 Add people who will ACT fast — not just people you know.
2
⚙️
Choose Your Trigger Method
Go to Settings and choose your trigger. Shake to Send — shake phone 3× in pocket, completely invisible, best for armed robbery. Silent SOS — press volume button 3×, no screen change. Fake Call — shows incoming call screen while SOS fires silently.⚠️ Open UnrugGuard BEFORE entering a risky area. The app must be open for triggers to work.
3
🔴
How the SOS Button Works
When SOS fires: 1) App gets your GPS location instantly. 2) Builds a Google Maps link. 3) Sends SMS to ALL contacts at once with your location and name. Works with zero data on any network worldwide.💡 If GPS fails the message still sends — always better to send than not send.
4
🔑
The Safe Word
Set a secret word — e.g. "Pineapple". If you are in danger but cannot press SOS, call a contact and say your safe word naturally in conversation. They know you are in danger without the attacker realising.💡 Tell your contacts the safe word BEFORE an emergency happens.
5
🌍
Emergency Numbers — Your Country
UnrugGuard automatically detects your country and shows the correct local emergency numbers on the Home screen. Tap any number to call instantly with zero data. Works worldwide.
6
🛡️
GuardNetwork — Live Location Sharing
Go to the Network tab and create your GuardNetwork ID — e.g. UG-KALU-7742. Share your ID with trusted contacts. When they add your ID and you approve, you can see each other's live location on a map inside the app. Requires internet.💡 GuardNetwork works independently from SOS. SOS always works even without internet.
7
🔋
Before Entering a Risky Area
✅ Open UnrugGuard
✅ Check contacts are saved
✅ Check trigger method is set
✅ Charge phone above 20%
✅ Tell one contact where you are going
✅ Know your safe word⚠️ This app cannot work if your phone is off or dead. Always charge before travelling.
