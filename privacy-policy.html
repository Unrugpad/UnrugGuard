Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
UnrugGuard is a free emergency safety app built to protect lives. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

UnrugGuard collects only the minimum information necessary to provide emergency safety features:

  • Location Data (GPS): Your device's GPS coordinates are collected when you open the app or press the SOS button. This is used to include your location in emergency alerts sent to your contacts.
  • GuardNetwork ID and Username: If you choose to use the GuardNetwork feature, we store your chosen username and a randomly generated ID on our servers to enable location sharing with trusted contacts.
  • Emergency Contacts: Contact names and phone numbers you enter are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Last Known Location: If you use GuardNetwork, your last known GPS coordinates are stored on our servers to enable location sharing with approved trusted contacts only.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your real name
  • We do NOT collect your email address
  • We do NOT collect payment information
  • We do NOT collect your phone number
  • We do NOT track your browsing history
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Sending your GPS location to emergency contacts when you press SOS
  • Enabling GuardNetwork live location sharing with people you have approved
  • Showing your last known location to approved GuardNetwork contacts
  • Delivering GuardNetwork SOS alerts to trusted contacts

4. Data Storage and Security

Your emergency contacts and personal settings are stored locally on your device and never leave your phone unless you choose to use GuardNetwork.

GuardNetwork data (username, ID, and location) is stored on Supabase servers hosted in Europe (Ireland) with industry-standard security measures.

We do not store your SMS messages or WhatsApp messages — those are sent directly from your device through your phone network.

5. Location Data

UnrugGuard requests location permission to include your GPS coordinates in SOS alerts. Location is only accessed:

  • When you open the app (to save your last known location)
  • When you press the SOS button
  • When you manually refresh your GuardNetwork location

We do not track your location in the background continuously.

6. GuardNetwork Data Sharing

Your location is only shared with people who:

  • Have the UnrugGuard app installed
  • Have sent you a connection request
  • Have been approved by you personally

You can remove any GuardNetwork connection at any time. When removed, that person can no longer see your location.

7. Third Party Services

UnrugGuard uses the following third party services:

  • Supabase — Database for GuardNetwork features. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy
  • DEXScreener API — For displaying UPAD and BNC token prices. No personal data is shared.
  • OpenStreetMap / ipapi.co — For country detection. No personal data is stored.

8. Children's Privacy

UnrugGuard is designed for users of all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require account creation or personal information to use its core SOS features.

9. Data Deletion

To delete your GuardNetwork data:

  • Contact us at unrugpad@gmail.com with your GuardNetwork ID
  • We will permanently delete all your data within 48 hours

Your locally stored data (contacts, settings) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of UnrugGuard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
